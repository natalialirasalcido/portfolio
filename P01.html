p 03 - Centro de las Etnias, 2021

La Matanza, Hermosillo, Sonora, México
Community Equipment
2021

 

 

We analized the reality experienced by the residents of La Matanza neighborhood and through an examination of community resources and dynamics, a connection was established with the values, customs, and traditions of the Seri ethnic group. This connection aimed to facilitate the development of an architectural proposal for an Ethnic Groups Center, intended to bring about positive transformation within the community.

 

Centro de las Etnias project draws inspiration from Seri handicrafts and arises from the necessity to foster a sense of identity among the inhabitants of La Matanza, who are members of the aforementioned ethnic group. This is achieved through the creation of a cultural center focused on Sonoran ethnic groups, wich was designed to address community needs and facilitate various functions arising from their sociocultural practices.

 

The project was guided by a range of criteria. These include creating an open and harmonious relationship with the environment, achieved through a well-structured circulation plan. Additionally, the project aims to incorporate ethnic groups without disrupting the natural surroundings. The design prioritizes maximum functionality, providing practical spaces for all user types. Lastly, an artistic intervention is integrated both inside and outside the center, enhancing the site's identity.
