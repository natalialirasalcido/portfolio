p 01 - Cinco, 2023

Colonia Cinco de Mayo, Hermosillo, Sonora, México
Mixed-use collective housing
2023

 

 

Cinco is a mixed-use project centered around sustainability that not only contributes to intra-urban consolidation and redensification, but also addresses the needs of the city and its residents. Cinco serves as an homage to the city, its traditions, and the neighborhood.

 

The project is situated on Cinco de Mayo, a neighborhood rich in tradition and identity where its residents have always been the cornerstone and the essence of the area.

 

Since the project's inception, several questions have emerged, such as: Is there something between the isolated suburban house and the 20-story urban skyscraper? Is there a concept of the "missing middle"? This involves medium-height blocks that offer both high density and a human scale simultaneously. This approach allows people to establish better connections with one another and their environment.

 

The success of the project also depends on resolving issues related to vertical housing acquisition and promoting mobility programs that reduce the car's prominence as the primary mode of transportation. Consequently, a redesign of the road network was proposed. This redesign incorporates bike lanes and sidewalks, enhancing pedestrian comfort and accessibility.
