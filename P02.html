p 04 - Casa Valdéz, 2021

Pomona Residencial, Hermosillo, Sonora, México
Single-family housing
2021

 

 

Casa Valdéz is a single-family housing project that focuses on developing an environmentally efficient housing prototype. This prototype makes use of the area's natural resources and engages users with nature through their senses.

 

The housing prototype is designed to be versatile, adapting to different types of users, terrains, or climates. It can combine several units to create new projects, pathways, or different layouts, all while ensuring a sustainable use of the land's resources without causing significant alterations. This is why it's considered an ideal option for a project located within a natural reserve.

 

By providing decent, affordable, and, above all, sustainable housing, the construction process promotes the responsible use of local natural resources.
